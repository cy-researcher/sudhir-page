## Research Interests
I am currently **researching** the application of **Graph Neural Networks (GNN)** to identify complex, non-linear patterns in transactional data for financial fraud detection. Additionally, my work explores **Federated Learning (FL)** frameworks to enable privacy-preserving machine learning in decentralized enterprise environments.

Previously, I have led global digital transformations at the enterprise level, focusing on **hardening organizational infrastructure** against automated threats and establishing rigorous AI governance standards.
